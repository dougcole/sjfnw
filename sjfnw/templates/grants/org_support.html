Applying for a grant

Eligibility
Please review our eligibility criteria before applying for a grant.
Application process
1. Register for an account
All this requires is an email, password, and your organization's name. Choose an email that you expect to have access to in future years.
2. Fill out the online form
  • Your home page has a links of Social Justice Fund grant cycles. Click the link on an open cycle to begin filling out the form.
  • Progress on the application is automatically saved every 30 seconds. You can close the window and find it later under "saved drafts" on your home page.
  • Upload documents at the end of the form: budget files, grant funding sources and diversity chart.

Accounts

Lost password
If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it. You'll need to enter the email that was used to register your organization - a link to continue the reset process will be sent to that address.
Forgotten email/login
If you forgot or no longer have access to the email used to register your organization, please contact us to have the email on your account updated.

Contact us

For questions related to Social Justice Fund's granting process, please see the information page for each grant cycle. You can find a link to this page on your home page for Social Justice Fund's online grant application.

For technical support, use our online form or send us an email at {{support_email}}.
